AMUMA-PSK
Dignity and sustainability for every family

Hope in Action

For over 40 years, Amuma-PSK (Pagtabang sa Kabus na Sugboanon) has been serving vulnerable communities in Cebu, Philippines. We bring hope in action by building dignity and sustainability for families through education, nutrition, healthcare, and community empowerment.

Addressing
Identifying and addressing immediate needs in education, nutrition, and healthcare for vulnerable families.
Integrating
Integrating comprehensive solutions that build stability and create sustainable livelihoods for families.
Reinforcing
Reinforcing community resilience through leadership development, advocacy, and empowerment initiatives.
