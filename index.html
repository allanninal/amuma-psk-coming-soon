AMUMA-PSK
Transforming lives in Cebu since 2019

Hope in Action

Founded by the Apostolic Sisters of St. John (ASSJ) in Cebu, Philippines, Paglaum Sakinabuhi (PSK) began as a mission to serve marginalized families in Banawa Hills. From addressing hunger to holistic development, we've evolved to meet both spiritual and material needs of children and families.

Our Mission

To provide transformative opportunities for marginalized children and their families, fostering holistic change through education, spiritual growth, and community building.

Our Vision

To inspire, equip, and empower marginalized children and their families, helping them thrive as responsible members of a community rooted in Christ.

Our Core Values

Faith

A personal response to God that cultivates trust in others.

Compassion

Being moved by the suffering of others and taking action to help them.

Growth

Embracing challenges, learning from experiences, and consistently pursuing opportunities to enhance skills and knowledge.

Our 2025-2031 Projects

AMUMA Project

A character building project focused on spiritual and human formation to cultivate strong values and growth in Catholic faith.

Programs: Faith Forward, Seeds of Greatness, Grow and Flourish

I CAN Project

Educational development and advancement opportunities through scholarships, literacy programs, and specialized training.

Programs: Hope for Tomorrow, Engage and Explore, Lead and Prosper

RISE UP Project

Capacity building for economic growth and stability through personal and professional development, vocational training, and social business initiatives.

Programs: Skills Up, Build Up, Thrive Up, Ground Up
